About us

Bike Shop, is a motorcycle company for motorcyclists. For 9 years, we have specialized in offering BMW motorcycle rental services, of the entire GS range, around the Mexican Republic. Our company is designed to satisfy all the needs of motorcycling enthusiasts, as well as those who start their adventure for the first time on one of their motorcycles. We want to share the excitement and adrenaline of traveling by motorcycle and touring our beautiful country and its surroundings.
